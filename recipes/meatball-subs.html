Mini Meatball Subs

How to make Mini Meatball Subs

These are great for a quick meal! A meatball with sauce and provolone cheese between a little roll! The best way to make these meatballs are to throw them in the crockpot with your favorite sauce and let them slow cook until you are ready for dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of Ground Beef
  • ½ Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of Chopped Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • Pasta Sauce
  • ½ Cup of Provolone Cheese
  • Dinner Rolls

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine all ingredients (except Pasta Sauce, Cheese and Dinner Rolls) combine, mix well and form into meatballs. Put meatballs in a baking dish or put meatballs in crockpot with Pasta Sauce
  3. Bake meatballs in the oven for 20 mins. Pour pasta sauce into a saucepan and bring to a simmer over a low heat. Stir in meatballs to the sauce. Simmer sauce until meatballs are fully cooked, ~20 mins. If slow cooking make sure you add the pasta sauce and meatballs at the same time.
  4. Place one meatball, cheese and a litle bit of sauce between eat roll and enjoy!

    Optional: Place sandwiches in hot oven, bake until cheese melts.
